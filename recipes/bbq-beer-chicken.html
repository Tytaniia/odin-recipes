Crockpot BBQ BeerChicken


The Best crockpot summer BBQ recipe!


Ingredients

  • 3 pounds skinless boneless chicken breast
    • 1 tbsp onion powder
  • 1 tbsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 8 oz pepper
  • 32 oz barbeque sauce

Directions

  1. Season chicken with onion powder, garlic powder, paprika, salt and pepper. Add to crockpot, then add beerand 24 oz bbq sauce.
  2. Cook on low for 8 hours, tossing once or teice if desired. After 8 hours, tossing once or twice if desired.
  3. After 8 hours, shred and add remaining bbq sauce. TOss chicken to coat and let sit for 10-15 minutes before serving.
