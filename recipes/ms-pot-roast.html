Mississippi Pot Roast

Best thing to have simmering on a cold winter morning!


Ingredients

  • 1 chuck roast
  • packet ranch dressing mix
  • 1 packet au jus gravy mix
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 4-5 pepperconi peppers

Directions

  1. Place roast in the slow cooker and sprinkle the top woth ranch dressing mix and au jus gravy mix. Place peppers on top of the mixes, and add butter
  2. Cook over low heat for heat for 8 hours. Serve with noodles rice or mashed potatoes. Enjoy!
