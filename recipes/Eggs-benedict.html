Eggs Benedict


Perfect Brunch food for lazy Sundays in


Ingredients

  • 4 english muffins
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 tbsp white vinegar
  • 1 pck of McCormick's Hollandaise Sauce
  • 8 Canadian bacon
  • chopped parsley
  • paprika

Directions

  1. Bring several inches of water along with vinegar just to boil in a large deep frying pan. Crack eggs one a time and add to boiling water, filling four eggs in pan at once.
  2. Cook untilwhites are just set and yolk is still runny, about 3-4 minutes. Transfer to paper towel lined plate to drain, repeat with remaining eggs.
  3. Cook canadian bacon in a skillet over medium-high heat until heated through, about 1 minute per side
  4. MEanwhile toast English muffins in a toaster oven or under broiler until golden brown on top.
  5. Lay a slice of canadian bacon atop each english muffin. Top each with a poached egg and season with salt and pepper. Spoon or pour sauce over top, garnish with parsley and paprika.
